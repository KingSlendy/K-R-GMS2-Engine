Asset Package themed around Crimson Needle 3.

Assets marked in Red are mandatory for other features to function.
Assets marked in Green are features of interest you might wish to add.
Assets marked in Yellow are cosmetic.



Additional information:
	- Updraft sprite has been modified to have a checkered sprite to match the other Vapor sprites.
	- Downwards Boosters have additional assets for Refreshing, and Cherry versions to match Upwards Boosters.
	- Gravity Blocks have 2 additional color variations for Right and Left gravity flippers.
